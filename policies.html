Policies

Simple policies for payments, artwork, and campaign timing.

This page is here to set expectations clearly before a campaign is booked. If anything in your order needs special handling, contact us before payment so we can confirm details in writing.

Payments

Deposits reserve placement.

A booking deposit secures postcard space and production planning. Final balances must be paid before mail preparation or release to print.

Refunds

Refunds depend on production stage.

Deposits may be non-refundable once layout work, route planning, or print preparation has started. If a campaign has already been approved for print, refunds may be limited or unavailable.

Artwork

Clients are responsible for submitted content.

Customers should provide accurate offers, prices, contact details, and any required rights to images or logos. Mailbox Merge is not responsible for third-party copyright issues in client-supplied materials.

Timing

Mail dates depend on approval and USPS timing.

Delivery windows can vary based on route selection, artwork approval, production timing, and USPS handling. Estimated dates are not guaranteed until materials and payment are fully cleared.

Contact

Questions before paying?

Contact Kingsley@mailboxmerge.com before submitting payment if you need special billing terms, updated route estimates, or campaign-specific production confirmation.
